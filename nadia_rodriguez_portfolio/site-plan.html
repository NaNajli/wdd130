About

I am a person who has learned to persevere and achieve my goals thanks to my efforts and the company of family and friends. I am currently a Brigham Young University Pathway student. I got to know the world of programming during the pandemic because the place where I worked closed; Therefore, I gave guitar classes to children and adolescents online for that reason I started to be interested in technology and used all the resources to be able to teach and motivate the children that I taught for almost a year. The first course I took was HTML and CSS at Codecademy. At the same time, I began to prepare myself to improve my level of English and be able to study at BYU. I am currently doing my first web and computer programming certificate at BYU. Some Hobbies In addition to my love of technology, design and code I am also interested in music. I have played guitar since I have sixteen years old it’s an activity that I do to relax and hope later to be able to use my knowledge in music to contribute to technology.

I know that all these skills that I have are valuable for teamwork and I am willing to continue learning in order to continue improving.
